Winter 2025 Important Updates

Key Deadlines and Events for Full-Term Classes at UMSI:

  • January 28: Add/Drop Deadline
    • Last day to drop a course with full tuition remission and without a "W" on your transcript. After this date, schedule changes must follow the Late Add/Drop procedure.
  • March 1 - March 9: Spring Break
    • No classes during Spring Break, but most University offices remain open to assist students.
  • March 19: Backpacking Begins
    • Students can add courses to their Backpack and view enrollment appointments to prepare for Fall 2025 registration. Refer to the Degree Progress and Registration page for guidance.
  • March 26 - March 28: Graduate Student Registration
    • Graduate students can begin registering for courses based on their enrollment appointments, available in Wolverine Access. Ensure that course schedules align with MSI degree requirements.

MSI Degree Requirements

The MSI Degree Requirements page provides essential information to help students understand the requirements for earning a Master of Science in Information (MSI) degree and create an academic plan.

  • Degree Completion Requirements:
    • Students must complete at least 48 credit hours of graduate coursework, with a minimum cumulative GPA of 3.0 (B average). At least 39 of these credit hours must be completed within UMSI (SI credit).
  • Key Terminology:
    • Students can view degree requirements and course planning resources based on their cohort (the term they started the program). Separate requirements apply to AMDP students, which can be found on the AMDP Degree Requirements page.
  • Dual Degree or Graduate Certificate:
    • MSI students may pursue a secondary degree or graduate certificate by applying and being admitted to the program. Additional information on requirements is provided on the page.
  • Additional Resources:
    • Students can access course search help and guidance through UMSI Academic Advising.

Course Waivers, Academic Policies, and Forms

The Course Waivers, Academic Policies, and Forms page serves as a resource for UMSI graduate students to navigate the course waiver process, understand relevant academic policies, and access necessary forms.

  • UMSI Course Waivers:
    • Students can waive specific courses commonly taken in the MSI program, allowing them to explore more advanced electives or other courses of interest.
    • Receiving a course waiver does not reduce the total credit requirements for the MSI degree. Students must still complete:
      • 39 SI credits.
      • 48 total graduate-level credits.
    • Details about course waivers, timelines, and processes are provided via the UMSI Course Waiver Site and the Course Waivers Canvas page.
  • Access to Academic Policies and Forms:
    • The page includes relevant policies and forms to help students successfully navigate their academic journey.
    • For more details or access to forms, students can contact Academic Success.
  • Contact Information:
    • Questions regarding course waivers can be directed to the UMSI Registrar Team at umsi.registrar@umich.edu.
    • For additional assistance, students can reach out to:
      • Academic Success at umsi.academicsuccess@umich.edu.
